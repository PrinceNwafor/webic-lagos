First Time at WEBIC

What to Expect

1
Arrive & Be Welcomed
You'll be greeted warmly by our hospitality team as you enter. We aim to make you feel at home from your first step through the door.
2
Experience Anointed Worship
Our music and choir team will lead you into a powerful time of praise and worship that creates an atmosphere for God's presence to move.
3
Receive the Word
Prophet Samson Ayorinde delivers a powerful, faith-building message directly from God's Word. Expect to be challenged, inspired, and transformed.
4
Prayers & Ministration
After the message, there is a time of prayer and ministration. Many have received healing, breakthroughs, and prophetic words during this time.
5
Connect With Us
First timers are invited to the Welcome Desk after the service to connect, ask questions, pick up resources, and learn about our ministries.

Notify Us You're Coming

Let us know in advance and we'll make sure someone is ready to welcome you personally.

How to Find Us

Getting to WEBIC

🚗

By Private Car

Take the Ogudu Road from Ojota or Mile 12. WEBIC is at 51-53 Ogudu Road, beside the Kosofe Local Government Secretariat. Parking is available on the church premises.

🚌

By Bus / Public Transport

Take a bus to Ojota or Mile 12, then a danfo or keke-napep to Ogudu Road. Tell the driver "WEBIC" or "Kosofe LGA." Many commercial buses pass by directly.
