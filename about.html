Our Story

We Are WEBIC

A church born from a divine mandate — to raise world evangelists, ignite miracles, and transform Lagos and the nations.

Founded With Purpose

How WEBIC Began

In 1996, Prophet (Dr.) Samson Ayorinde, a trained architect with a master's degree from the University of Ilorin and a former lecturer at Ahmadu Bello University (ABU), answered a divine call. God redirected his path from academia into full-time ministry with one powerful mandate: "Go, and evangelize the world."

What began as a small counselling unit with barely 100 worshippers grew, under God's hand, into one of the most vibrant Pentecostal churches in Nigeria. The Tuesday Miracle Service became a landmark event — drawing thousands from Lagos and across the country every week.

Today, WEBIC has over 32 branches in Lagos alone, with additional branches in Abuja, Ilorin, Port Harcourt, and internationally in Europe, Asia, and the USA — all anchored by the headquarters at 51-53 Ogudu Road, Lagos.

Prophet (Dr.) Samson Ayorinde is one of Nigeria's most respected prophetic voices. With a background in Architecture (MSc, University of Ilorin) and a stint as a lecturer at Ahmadu Bello University, God interrupted his academic career with a divine call into full-time ministry in the mid-1990s.

He founded the Tuesday Miracle Service — which quickly became legendary across Nigeria for documented healings, deliverances, and divine interventions. The frequency of testimonies surrounding barrenness has earned him the affectionate title "Barrenness Bulldozer" — a testament to the hundreds of couples who have received the fruit of the womb through his ministry.

Under his leadership, WEBIC has expanded from a single congregation in Ogudu to over 32 branches in Lagos, multiple branches in Abuja, Ilorin, and Port Harcourt, and international presence in Europe, Asia, and the USA.

Prophet Ayorinde is known not just for his prophetic anointing, but for his deep passion for souls — his crusades and outreach programs have reached millions across Nigeria. His ministry is a living proof that God still uses ordinary men to do extraordinary things.

Our Journey

Milestones of Grace

1996
The Beginning
Prophet Samson Ayorinde obeys God's call and starts WEBIC with a small counselling unit of about 100 worshippers in Ogudu, Lagos.
1998
Miracle Service Launched
The Tuesday Miracle Service is established, quickly drawing thousands from across Lagos and surrounding states seeking healing, deliverance, and miracles.
2005
Expansion Across Lagos
WEBIC begins rapid multiplication, planting branches across Lagos — reaching Ajah, Ikeja, Surulere, Lekki, and beyond.
2010
National & International Presence
Branches established in Abuja, Ilorin, and Port Harcourt. International expansion begins with congregations in Europe and the USA.
2025
29 Years & Still Going Strong
WEBIC celebrates 29 years of unbroken ministry with 32+ branches in Lagos, a global online audience, and thousands receiving miracles weekly.
