Our Heart

A Church Where
Miracles Still Happen

Since 1996, WEBIC has been a place of supernatural encounters, healing, and radical transformation under the anointed ministry of Prophet (Dr.) Samson Ayorinde.

What started with 100 worshippers has grown into thousands who gather weekly to experience God's presence — in Lagos, across Nigeria, and around the world. Whether you're seeking healing, breakthrough, or simply a spiritual home, you are welcome here.

General Overseer

Prophet (Dr.)
Samson Ayorinde

Known across Nigeria and beyond as a prophet of uncommon grace, Prophet Samson Ayorinde founded WEBIC in 1996 with a mandate from God to raise an army of world evangelists.

An architect by training (MSc, University of Ilorin), God redirected his path into full-time ministry. His Tuesday Miracle Services became legendary for testimonies of healing, deliverance, and divine intervention — particularly for those believing God for the fruit of the womb. He is fondly called the "Barrenness Bulldozer" by his congregation.
