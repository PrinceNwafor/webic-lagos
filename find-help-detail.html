A Word For You

If you are reading this page today, it means there is a need in your life that you have brought to God. That step of faith is not small — it is everything.

WEBIC believes in a God who heals. Not a God who used to heal, or who heals some but not others. The same Jesus who healed the leper, the blind man, the woman with the issue of blood — He is the same yesterday, today, and forever (Hebrews 13:8). Your case is not too hard for Him.

Prophet Samson Ayorinde has ministered healing to thousands of people across Nigeria and beyond. At our Miracle Services held every Tuesday, the atmosphere is charged for supernatural intervention. We invite you to come — but even if you cannot come physically, your prayer request reaches the same God.

How We Pray With You

Our intercessors cover these specific areas when they lift your name before the Lord.

A Testimony From This Category

I had fibroid for six years and the doctors told me I needed surgery. I came to the WEBIC Miracle Service and Prophet Ayorinde prayed specifically for women with conditions in the womb. Three weeks later my scan came back completely clear. No surgery needed. Jesus did it — all glory to God!

Sister Ngozi Okafor — Healing
