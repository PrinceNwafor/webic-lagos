Our Ministries

There is a place for you at WEBIC. From youth to women, evangelism to prayer — find your community and your purpose.

Find Your Place

Every Ministry. One Family.

WEBIC is made up of vibrant ministries that serve every age group, gender, and life stage. Find where you belong.

🔥
Evangelism Ministry

The heartbeat of WEBIC. Our evangelism teams go into the streets, markets, hospitals, and across Nigeria carrying the Gospel with signs following. Join us in fulfilling the Great Commission.

🙏
Prayer Ministry

The engine room of WEBIC. Our intercessors are the backbone of every miracle, breakthrough, and transformation in the church. Join us for early morning prayers, all-night vigils, and prayer chains.

👦👧
WEBIC Youth Ministry

Raising a generation that is bold, anointed, and unashamed of the Gospel. Our youth programs include Bible study, outreach, sports, mentorship, and annual camps. Ages 13–30.

👶
Children's Church

Training children in the way of God from a young age. Our dedicated children's workers create a safe, fun, and Spirit-filled environment where kids can learn, worship, and grow every Sunday.

👩‍👩‍👧
Women's Fellowship

A community of faith-filled, purpose-driven women. Through monthly meetings, prayer, empowerment programs, and outreaches, WEBIC women are equipped for impact in their homes and society.

👨‍👨‍👦
Men's Fellowship

Raising kingdom-minded men of prayer, integrity, and vision. Our Men's Fellowship meets regularly for Bible study, mentorship, leadership development, and community outreach.

🎶
Music & Choir Ministry

Ushering in God's presence through anointed worship and praise. Our choir and music team minister every service with excellence, creating an atmosphere where miracles happen.

🎓
Campus Fellowship

Taking the fire of God to the campuses of Lagos and Nigeria. WEBIC has a strong student fellowship that targets universities, polytechnics, and colleges with the Gospel.

💍
Marriage & Family Ministry

Building strong homes on the Word of God. Marriage counselling, pre-marital courses, family enrichment seminars, and parenting workshops — for couples at every stage of life.
