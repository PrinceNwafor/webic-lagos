Our Prayer Process

How We Pray With You

Every prayer request that comes to WEBIC is taken seriously. Here is what happens when you reach out.

1
You Submit Your Request
Use the form below, WhatsApp, or call us directly. Share as much or as little as you are comfortable with.
2
Our Prayer Team Intercedes
Trained intercessors pray over your request — individually, not just in bulk. Your name is lifted before God.
3
Prophet Ayorinde Is Informed
Special requests are brought to the attention of our General Overseer for prophetic intercession and counsel.
4
We Follow Up With You
We check back to stand in faith with you and celebrate when your miracle comes through — because it will.
Prayer Categories

What Do You Need Prayer For?

God specialises in impossible situations. Choose the area where you need His touch today.

🙏
Healing & Health
Whether it is a chronic illness, a sudden diagnosis, or a loved one on a hospital bed — Jesus still heals. He bore our sicknesses on the cross and by His stripes we are healed (Isaiah 53:5).
Pray for Healing
🌿
Fruitfulness & Barrenness
WEBIC has a strong anointing for the fruit of the womb. Prophet Ayorinde has ministered to thousands who are now parents. If you have been waiting on God for children, there is a word for you here.
Pray for Fruitfulness
⚡
Breakthrough & Open Doors
Are you stuck? Has favour eluded you in business, career, education, or ministry? God is able to open doors that no man can shut. Your season of breakthrough is here.
Pray for Breakthrough
🔓
Deliverance & Freedom
Oppression, spiritual bondage, generational patterns, and demonic harassment do not have the final say. Jesus came to set the captives free, and at WEBIC we believe in complete deliverance through His name.
Pray for Deliverance
💑
Marriage & Relationships
Are you trusting God for a life partner? Is your marriage going through a difficult season? God is the author of covenant and He can restore, heal, and bring together what belongs together.
Pray for Marriage
💼
Financial Breakthrough
Debt, unemployment, business failure, and financial lack are not your portion. The God of more than enough is able to make all grace abound toward you so that you have everything you need (2 Corinthians 9:8).
Pray for Provision
🕊️
Grief & Loss
The loss of a loved one, a broken relationship, or a shattered dream can feel unbearable. God is close to the broken-hearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit (Psalm 34:18). You do not have to grieve alone.
Find Comfort
✝️
Salvation for a Loved One
Do you have a family member, friend, or colleague who does not know Jesus? The Lord is not willing that any should perish. Your intercession for their soul is powerful and effective.
Pray for Their Soul
🧭
Direction & Guidance
Standing at a crossroads in life — career, ministry, relocation, a major decision? God promises to direct your steps when you acknowledge Him. Let us seek His face together for clarity and wisdom.
Seek God's Direction
