What We Stand On

Our Beliefs

WEBIC is rooted in the full Gospel — the Bible as the Word of God, salvation through Christ alone, the baptism of the Holy Spirit, divine healing, and the imminent return of Jesus.

Statement of Faith

What We Believe

WEBIC is a Pentecostal church that holds firmly to the full counsel of God's Word. These are the foundational truths that guide everything we do.

01
The Holy Bible
We believe the Holy Bible — both Old and New Testaments — is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is our final rule of faith and practice in all matters of life and doctrine (2 Timothy 3:16-17).
02
The Holy Trinity
We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — co-equal, co-eternal, and co-existent. Each person of the Godhead is distinct yet one in nature, substance, and essence (Matthew 28:19; 2 Corinthians 13:14).
03
Salvation Through Jesus Christ
We believe that salvation is available to all who repent of their sins and receive Jesus Christ as Lord and Saviour. Jesus is the only way, the only truth, and the only life — there is no other name under heaven by which we must be saved (John 3:16; Acts 4:12; Romans 10:9-10).
04
The Baptism of the Holy Spirit
We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in other tongues as the Spirit gives utterance (Acts 2:4). This gift is available to every believer and empowers the Church for witnessing and service. We believe the gifts of the Holy Spirit are for the Church today.
05
Divine Healing
We believe that healing — physical, emotional, and spiritual — is provided in the atonement of Jesus Christ and is available for all believers today. God still heals the sick, opens the womb, and performs miracles in direct answer to prayer and faith (Isaiah 53:5; Matthew 8:17; James 5:14-15).
06
Water Baptism
We believe in water baptism by immersion as an outward declaration of an inward transformation. Every believer who has received Jesus Christ is instructed to be baptised in water as an act of obedience and public confession of their faith (Matthew 28:19; Romans 6:3-4).
07
The Lord's Supper (Holy Communion)
We believe in the Lord's Supper as a memorial of Christ's death and a proclamation of His return. Believers are to partake regularly in remembrance of the sacrifice of Jesus, discerning His body and blood (1 Corinthians 11:23-26).
08
The Prophetic Ministry
We believe in the prophetic ministry as one of the fivefold gifts given to the Church for her edification, exhortation, and comfort (1 Corinthians 14:3; Ephesians 4:11-12). Prophecy must always align with Scripture and be tested.
09
Holiness & Christian Living
We believe that every born-again believer is called to a life of holiness, righteousness, and separation from sin. The Holy Spirit enables believers to overcome sin and live victoriously. Without holiness, no one will see the Lord (Hebrews 12:14; 1 Peter 1:15-16).
10
The Second Coming of Christ
We believe in the imminent, personal, bodily return of Jesus Christ to the earth. He will return first for His Church (the Rapture), and then in glory to reign on earth for a thousand years. We are to live each day with the expectation of His return (1 Thessalonians 4:16-17; Revelation 19:11-16).
11
Evangelism & World Missions
We believe that the Great Commission — to go into all the world and preach the Gospel to every creature — is the primary mandate of the Church. Every believer is called to be a witness for Christ, and WEBIC is committed to raising world evangelists until Jesus returns (Matthew 28:18-20; Mark 16:15-18).

"For I am not ashamed of the gospel of Christ: for it is the power of God unto salvation to every one that believeth."

Romans 1:16 (KJV)
